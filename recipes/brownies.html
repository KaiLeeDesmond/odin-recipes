Brownies

Description

This is a relatively simple brownie recipe with around 8 ingredients or so.

It has a relatively short baking time of 30 minutes, requires minimal preperation and is easy to follow along.


Ingredients

  • Butter: 10 tbsp
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 2 tsp
  • Eggs: 2
  • Milk Chocolate Chips: 1.5 cups
  • Cocoa Powder: 1/4 cup
  • Flour: 3/4 cup
  • Salt: 1/2 tsp

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on low heat. Make sure not to burn it!
    Brown Butter and Brownies may sound good on paper, but it won't taste as good.
  2. Blend the sugar and whisk it with the melted butter until smooth.
  3. Next, add the eggs and the vanilla extract to the wet mix and whisk until combined.
  4. Gently melt 1/2 cup of milk chocolate chips and add it to the wet mix.
    Whisk until combined.
  5. Add the flour and cocoa powder to the wet mix and fold with a spatula until smooth.
  6. Add the remaining milk chocolate chips (1 cup) to the mix and fold with a spatula.
  7. Add the mixture to a lined 9x9 pan.
  8. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes.
    Remember to preheat the oven beforehand!
  9. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan for 30 minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
