Blue Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Cookies inspired by the classic Sesame Street Menace: The Cookie Monster.

This cookie recipe is also great for Percy Jackson fans.


Ingredients

  • Butter: 1.5 sticks
  • Brown Sugar: 3/4 cup
  • White Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 tbsp
  • Eggs:2 (One egg and one egg yolk)
  • Blue Food Coloring: As Much as you Want!
  • Flour: 2.25 cups
  • Cornstarch: 1.5 tsp
  • Baking Soda: 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Milk Chocolate Chips: 1 cup
  • White Chocolate Chips: 1 cup
  • Hershey's Chocolate: One Bar

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on low heat. Make sure not to burn it!
    Brown Butter and Blue Chocolate Chip Cookies may sound good on paper, but it won't taste as good.
  2. Add the brown sugar followed by the white sugar and mix with a spatula until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Next, add 1 whole egg and 1 egg yolk to the wet mix and mix until combined.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and the desired amount of blue food coloring until the blue is uniform throughout the wet mix.
  5. Add the flour, cornstarch, baking soda and salt (in that order) and add it to the wet mix.
    Whisk until combined.
  6. Add the milk chocolate chips and the white chocolate chips to the dough and fold.
  7. Pat out some dough into a big ball and some Hershey's chocolate in the center.
  8. Allow the dough balls to chill for four hours.
  9. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Line large baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.
  10. Bake the cookies for 12-14 minutes or until the edges are very lightly browned.
  11. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
